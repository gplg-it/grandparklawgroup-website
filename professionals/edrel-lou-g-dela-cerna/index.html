About

A dedicated paralegal with a strong foundation in legal support, combining precision, initiative, and a client-centered approach to advance the firm’s work.

With one year of hands-on experience as a paralegal, Edrel Lou Dela Cerna provides essential litigation and administrative support to the firm’s legal team. She assists in case management, drafting and reviewing legal documents, coordinating filings and service, and maintaining clear communication with clients and opposing counsel. Her work is marked by attention to detail, organization, and a strong understanding of procedural requirements, enabling attorneys to move cases forward efficiently and effectively.

Edrel approaches her role with professionalism and adaptability, balancing accuracy with responsiveness in a fast-paced legal environment. She is known for her reliability and ability to manage multiple matters simultaneously while maintaining high standards of quality and confidentiality. Her growing experience has allowed her to develop a practical, solutions-oriented mindset that supports both the firm’s attorneys and its clients.

Driven by a genuine interest in the legal profession, Edrel is committed to continuous learning and professional growth. She values collaboration and clear communication, and she takes pride in contributing meaningfully to each case she supports. As she continues to expand her skills and experience, she remains focused on delivering dependable legal support and upholding the firm’s commitment to excellence.
