Bio

A fierce advocate for employees facing workplace injustice, Jennifer Martinez has dedicated her career to holding employers accountable and securing meaningful remedies for workers across California.

Jennifer L. Martinez is a Partner at Grand Park Law Group, where she leads the firm's employment law and civil rights practice. With over 15 years of experience, she has successfully represented thousands of employees in cases involving wrongful termination, discrimination, harassment, wage and hour violations, and retaliation.

Ms. Martinez has recovered over $50 million in verdicts and settlements for her clients, including several landmark cases that have shaped California employment law. She is known for her tenacious advocacy, meticulous preparation, and ability to translate complex legal issues into compelling narratives for juries.

Representative matters include:

  • Rodriguez v. TechCorp Industries: $8.5 million jury verdict in racial discrimination and wrongful termination case against Fortune 500 technology company.
  • Chen v. Pacific Healthcare Systems: $4.2 million settlement in sexual harassment and retaliation case involving hospital administration.
  • Martinez v. ABC Staffing (Class Action): $12 million settlement for wage and hour violations affecting over 2,000 workers.

Prior to joining Grand Park Law Group, Ms. Martinez served as a trial attorney at the U.S. Equal Employment Opportunity Commission, where she prosecuted systemic discrimination cases against major employers. This experience gave her unique insight into regulatory enforcement and the strategies employers use to defend against discrimination claims.

Ms. Martinez is a frequent speaker at legal conferences and has been quoted in the Los Angeles Times, Bloomberg Law, and Law360 on employment law developments. She serves on the board of the California Employment Lawyers Association and volunteers with local legal aid organizations.
